Active Buzzer Module

Overview

The active buzzer module provides alert sounds.

Purpose in the Project

- Generates warning alerts.
- Indicates obstacle detection.

Technical Specifications

Parameter| Details
Operating Voltage| 3.3V – 5V
Type| Active Buzzer

Working Principle

The buzzer produces sound when triggered by the NodeMCU.

Advantages

- Easy integration.
- Low power consumption.
- Loud audio alerts.
