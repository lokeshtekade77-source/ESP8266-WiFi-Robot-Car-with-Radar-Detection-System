Battery Power Supply

Overview

The battery supplies electrical power to all robot components.

Purpose in the Project

- Powers motors and NodeMCU.
- Provides stable system operation.

Technical Specifications

Parameter| Details
Type| Li-ion Battery
Voltage| 7.4V
Cell Type| 18650

Working Principle

The battery provides power to the L298N motor driver and NodeMCU through regulated voltage.

Advantages

- Rechargeable.
- Portable.
- High energy density.
