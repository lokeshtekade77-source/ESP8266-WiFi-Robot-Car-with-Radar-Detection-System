L298N Motor Driver Module

Overview

The L298N motor driver controls the movement of DC motors.

Purpose in the Project

- Controls motor direction.
- Enables speed control.

Technical Specifications

Parameter| Details
Operating Voltage| 5V – 35V
Channels| Dual H-Bridge
Output Current| 2A per channel

Pin Connections

L298N Pin| NodeMCU Pin
IN1| D1
IN2| D2
IN3| D7
IN4| D8

Working Principle

The module receives low-current signals from the NodeMCU and drives the DC motors for forward and backward movement.

Advantages

- Supports dual motors.
- Easy motor direction control.
- Suitable for robotics applications.
