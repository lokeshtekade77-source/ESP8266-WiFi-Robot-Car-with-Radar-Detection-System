SG90 Micro Servo Motor

Overview

The SG90 micro servo motor rotates the ultrasonic sensor for radar scanning.

Purpose in the Project

- Rotates the ultrasonic sensor.
- Enables radar-like obstacle detection.

Technical Specifications

Parameter| Details
Operating Voltage| 4.8V – 6V
Rotation Angle| 180°
Control Signal| PWM

Pin Connections

Servo Pin| NodeMCU Pin
VCC| 5V
GND| GND
Signal| D4

Working Principle

The servo motor rotates to specific angles using PWM signals from the NodeMCU.

Advantages

- Lightweight and compact.
- Accurate angle positioning.
- Low power consumption.
