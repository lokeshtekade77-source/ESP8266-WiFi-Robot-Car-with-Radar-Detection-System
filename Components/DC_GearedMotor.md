DC Geared Motors (BO Motors)

Overview

DC geared motors provide movement to the robot car.

Purpose in the Project

- Enables forward and backward motion.
- Supports robot navigation.

Technical Specifications

Parameter| Details
Operating Voltage| 3V – 12V
Type| BO Motor
Speed| 100 RPM

Working Principle

The motors rotate wheels using electrical energy supplied from the motor driver.

Advantages

- Good torque.
- Lightweight.
- Affordable and reliable.
